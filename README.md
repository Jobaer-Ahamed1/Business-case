# Business Case 03

This business case outlines the insights derived from the Power BI dashboard, focusing on customer order trends, restaurant performance, revenue analysis, rider performance, and customer engagement metrics.

## Key Insights:

1. **Customer Order Trends**: Analysis of changing customer preferences and peak ordering times across different demographics.

2. **Restaurant Performance**: Overview of performance metrics, including average meal preparation time and delivery times for different restaurant partners.

3. **Revenue Analysis**: Detailed examination of revenue streams, specifically highlighting the Biryani dish contributing approximately 17.05K in revenue.

4. **Rider Performance**: Evaluation of rider efficiency and delivery success rates, leading to actionable recommendations for improvement.

5. **Customer Engagement Metrics**: Insights into customer interactions with the platform, identifying trends in repeat customers and feedback ratings. 


## Conclusion

Implementing the insights gained from this analysis can significantly enhance operational efficiency and optimize menu offerings to align with customer demands.
