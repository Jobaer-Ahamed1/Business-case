# Business Case 02

This business case provides a comprehensive analysis of sales performance, product inventory management, and customer purchasing behaviors. The insights are derived from detailed dashboards and reports covering:

1. **Sales Category Analysis**: Identification of top-performing and underperforming product categories, with Electronics leading at 49.60K in sales while Clothing represents the weakest category at 3.67K.

2. **Product Inventory Management**: Real-time assessment of stock levels and availability status across product categories, enabling data-driven inventory optimization.

3. **Customer Order Patterns**: Analysis of weakest-performing products by total orders and customer purchase frequency, identifying trends in multi-month purchasing behavior.

4. **Top Customers & Products**: Evaluation of high-value customers and best-selling products (Smartphone: 28K, Laptop: 22K) to prioritize marketing and sales efforts.

5. **Temporal Sales Trends**: Monthly sales breakdown for 2024 showing peak selling periods, with November 2024 recording 16.48K and current month (January 2026) at 24.44K in total sales.

6. **Performance Metrics**: Three-month sales comparison and status tracking to monitor business trajectory and identify growth or decline patterns.

This analysis enables strategic decision-making for inventory allocation, pricing strategies, and targeted customer engagement initiatives.
